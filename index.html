Polling URL Format:

https://hossain-khan.github.io/trmnl-kung-fu-panda-quotes/api/random-quote-##{{ theme }}.json

Theme Selection: Users can choose from 9 themes in TRMNL plugin settings:

  • All Themes (default) - Mixed quotes from all categories
  • Wisdom - Philosophical quotes from Master Oogway, Shifu
  • Humor - Funny moments and comedic quotes
  • Growth - Self-improvement and personal development
  • Combat - Action-packed kung fu quotes
  • Identity - Finding yourself and your purpose
  • Confidence - Believing in yourself
  • Iconic - Most memorable lines
  • Villainy - Quotes from the antagonists

Quote History Tracking: Automatically prevents repeat quotes within 30 days. The system tracks displayed quotes and ensures you get fresh content each day, with smart fallback when all quotes have been shown.

Refresh Rate: 1440 minutes (24 hours) - Get a new quote every day

Available Layouts:

  • Full - Quote with movie poster (full screen)
  • Half Horizontal - Quote and character details side-by-side
  • Half Vertical - Quote and details stacked vertically
  • Quadrant - Minimal quote-only display
